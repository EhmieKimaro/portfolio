Portfolio Details

The AI Conversational Kiosk is an innovative solution designed to enhance the self-service experience in supermarkets and malls. By integrating AI-powered conversational capabilities, the kiosk allows for seamless and intuitive interactions during checkout.

Design Thinking Process

The project began with user research to understand pain points in self-service systems. Wireframes and prototypes were created to design a user-friendly interface that supports both voice and text-based interactions. Iterative testing ensured the AI responses were accurate and user-focused.
