Portfolio Details

The Booka App is a children's book app which was designed for kids aged 5 to 12, encouraging independent reading through an engaging, safe, and interactive platform. It features a library of age-appropriate books. I was supposed to add gamified challenges and progress tracking to make learning enjoyable. But i am yet to reach that phase of the design. I just focused on one case scenerio which was having a library of books for children to enjoy online.

Design Thinking Process

1. Empathize: Conducted user research with parents and children to identify needs like fun visuals, simple navigation, and secure features.

2. Define: Framed the problem: "How might we make reading exciting and accessible for children?"

3. Ideate: Brainstormed features like personalized recommendations, voiceovers, animations, and parental controls.

4. Prototype: Created wireframes and high-fidelity designs with playful colors, interactive storytelling, and gamification.

5. Test: Tested with users and iterated based on feedback, improving navigation, readability, and accessibility.

Outcome

The app combines education with entertainment, offering a child-friendly interface and features like a “Read Along” mode, rewards for reading, and a secure parental dashboard. It’s an engaging tool to promote literacy and foster a love for books.
