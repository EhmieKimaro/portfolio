About Me

UX Designer passionate about creating meaningful, intuitive digital experiences that bridge business goals and user needs. Skilled in Figma, prototyping, and usability testing, with a focus on designing inclusive, human-centered products that enhance everyday interactions.

Tools I am comfortable working with: Figma, Miro, FigJam, Sketch, Trello etc.

Areas of interest: AI design, product design, service design, UX research.

Work preference: Open to relocation and international opportunities (on-site, remote or hybrid).

My Approach

I design with empathy, curiosity, and clarity. My process combines research, strategy, and creativity to uncover real user needs and turn them into meaningful experiences. From early discovery to testing and iteration, I focus on designing for inclusion, usability, and impact. I enjoy exploring how emerging technologies like AI can enhance everyday interactions - whether it’s simplifying self-checkout experiences, connecting digital and physical shopping, or improving accessibility in design.

What Sets Me Apart

What makes me stand out is my ability to balance analytical thinking with creative problem-solving. I take a end-to-end view of user experience - not just how something looks, but how it works and feels. My projects often blend research depth, cross-disciplinary collaboration, and thoughtful prototyping, showing that good design isn’t just about visuals but about creating value and connection. I’m adaptable, proactive, and genuinely excited to keep learning - always seeking ways to design products that make life simpler and more human.
